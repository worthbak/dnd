# Session 25 - Intelligence Gathering in Everlund

*Sat, 28 Mar 26*

### Session Recap

- Recapped session 24: party declined Asmodeus's contract, he activated the Margasters as backup agents
- Asmodeus pulled the Margasters' protections back into Hell — literally set their Silverymoon estate on fire with brimstone
- Party escaped via Velaxis's chariot; Margasters escaped separately via flash of light from carriage house (likely teleportation)
- Xamlyn Margaster was seen grabbing a satchel of documents before fleeing
- Party confirmed at Level 9

### Party Strategy Discussion

- Identified Duke Zalto and the Ember Crown as the most immediate threat
  - Zalto doesn't understand the Crown is a seal artifact — views it as a power source for the Vonindod
  - Using it to power the Vonindod could destabilize the seal
- Key insight (Zerai, nat 20 on Asmodeus): he works through agents on the material plane with limited power compared to Nessus; he wouldn't send the Margasters out without good intel — wherever they're going is probably where the party should go too
- Discussed that collecting artifacts in one place is dangerous — need a stash plan; the Watcher at the Eye of the Allfather was suggested as a possible custodian
- Decided to start by gathering intelligence in Everlund

### Arrival in Everlund

- Velaxis dropped the party at Moongleam Tower rooftop; Harper guards alarmed by the hell-chariot but recognized the party's Harper pins
- Velaxis departed (left a "sending stone" — defecated on the floor; Roy collected both turds)
- Nightmare horses remained behind, disenchanted
- Dafis introduced himself to the group; party clocked his Eladrin nature (green hair, light skin — spring season); Roy's centaur disguise held up under scrutiny

### Moongleam Tower — Meeting with Krowen

**Tower Security:**
- Moongleam Tower on full lockdown — moat flooded, heavy interior security, guards on roof
- Response to two prior Zhentarim security breaches (Xolkin infiltration and subsequent assassination)
- Krowen cannot offer Harper forces due to lockdown — only basic supplies and healing potions

**What The Party Told Krowen:**
- Disclosed the Seal of Six: six artifacts, the Unbound, the Ordning's shattering removing magical protections, the conch in Asmodeus's possession
- Dafis revealed himself as a member of the Order of the Sixth Watch — Krowen recognized the Order from an old book in his library; assumed it was extinct

**What Krowen Told Us:**
- Duke Zalto appears to be consolidating fire giant forces at Ironslag
  - Fire giant activity has decreased across the region — suggests consolidation, not expansion
  - Zalto was seeking a Zhentarim alliance to help with the Vonindod; the party's disruption of that deal (session 18) set him back significantly
  - Zalto is now looking for other means of assistance — missing some key component to make the Vonindod work
- Connected us to Horix Zoar, a member of the Council of Elders and enemy of the Margasters

**Items Received:**
- Dafis: Boots of Elvenkind (advantage on Stealth) and Gloves of Thievery (+5 Sleight of Hand)
- Shelf learned all six Harper teleportation circle sigils: Everlund, Mirabar, Yartar, Loudwater, Waterdeep, Neverwinter
- Harper pins issued to Dafis and Norble

### Hall of the Elders — Meeting with Horix Zoar

**Who He Is:**
- Captain of the Everlund Guard, Council of Elders member
- Zoar family was pushed out of Waterdeep by the Margasters — accused of devil worship, which Horix claims was the Margasters projecting their own corruption
- Deeply bitter, foul-mouthed, pirate energy; barely 5ft tall with a mad-eye-moody bulging eye, covered in leather with a huge scimitar
- Dafis's spider familiar scouted his office — found angsty journals about the family's exile from Waterdeep; office barely used, books collecting dust except for piracy histories

**What Horix Told Us:**
- Margasters likely have teleportation circles in multiple locations:
  - Possibly Waterdeep (logical but conspicuous)
  - **Thornhold**: Margaster ancestral fortress north of Waterdeep, currently occupied by dwarves
    - Family has been scheming to reclaim it for centuries
    - Horix suspects they have a teleportation circle there
    - Rumored entrance to the Underdark beneath Thornhold
    - If Margasters needed a fallback after losing Silverymoon, Thornhold is the logical choice
- **Orthovier in Triboar** is a Margaster defector — the younger brother of Xamlyn Margaster
  - Would have the most specific knowledge of where Xamlyn would flee
  - Horix suggested we leverage any existing relationship with him

**Items Received:**
- Dafis: +2 Maul with one daily charge of a 1st-level Paladin spell (choosing spell later)
- Snuff powder partaken (haste-like effect, 1 minute)
- Horix considers us allies in his anti-Margaster crusade

### Departure

- Teleported from Moongleam Tower to Yartar via Harper circle
- Next destination: Triboar to find Orthovier
  - Confirmed we still have Orthovier's adamantine dagger (original quest item from session 2)
  - Plan: return the dagger, build goodwill, extract intelligence on Margaster movements and Thornhold

### Key Questions Going Forward

- Where exactly did the Margasters teleport to? Thornhold is the strongest lead
- What is the Margasters' relationship to Duke Zalto? Are they heading to Ironslag or somewhere else?
- What key component is Zalto missing for the Vonindod?
- Should we pursue Thornhold/Underdark or go directly to Ironslag?
- Can we pose as mercenary agents to infiltrate Zalto's operation?
