### Crystal Leviathan Fight

- Massive crystal-enhanced octopus — four tentacles with crystalline growths, crystal crown drilled into skull
- High initiative all around (Dagger of Warning advantage): Vethna 25, Tav 23, Oz 20, Dolrum 20
- Key moments:
	- Tav opened with smoke powder arrow (20 damage)
	- Crystal Pulse AoE: 29 necrotic, DC 17 Con — devastated the party
	- Psychic Lance on Oz (halved to 9 via Uncanny Dodge, first use) then on Tav
	- Leviathan threw Dolrum at Oz as legendary action
	- **Tav's big turn:** Hunter's Mark + Tempest Lash = 52 damage, severed two tentacles — then Psychic Lance knocked her unconscious
	- Oz sniped a tentacle from the hanging cage (Sheila Retribution, 23 damage)
	- Tana grappled, dragged to maw — saved on exactly DC 18 with 1 HP
- **Dolrum's rescue:**
	- Oz spotted pulsing light at pool bottom
	- Dolrum swan-dived in (Athletics 21), found Vapori chained at bottom with crystalline harness
	- **Nat 20** on chains — freed hands and shattered neck crystals in one blow
	- Second attack freed feet — all crystals on Leviathan crumbled immediately
	- Dolrum + Vapori touched the creature, burned Heroic Inspiration → **nat 20** Animal Handling
	- Leviathan fled through underwater passage — alive, possibly future ally
